Dr. Manju Khemani

Gynaecologist and Obstetrician

Dr. Manju Khemani is a Laparoscopic Surgeon (Obs & Gyn),Infertility Specialist and Obstetrician in Saket, Delhi and has an experience of 45 years in these fields. Dr. Manju Khemani practices at Max Smart Superspeciality Hospital in Saket, Delhi. She completed MBBS from Gandhi Medical College, Bhopal in 1978 and MD - Obstetrics & Gynaecology from Gandhi Medical College, Bhopal in 1981. She is a member of Delhi Medical Council and Medical Council of India (MCI).
